About

BIOGRAPHY

Julia Do is a Vietnamese-American multidisciplinary artist and writer based in Portland, Oregon. Her work explores themes of cultural identity, memory, and the natural world through a variety of mediums.

With a background in both fine arts and literature, Julia approaches each project with a storyteller's sensibility, whether working with paint, photography, or prose.

Julia's work has been exhibited in galleries across the Pacific Northwest and featured in numerous literary journals. She holds an MFA from the Pacific Northwest College of Art.

When not creating, Julia teaches art workshops for underserved youth and volunteers with organizations promoting arts education.
